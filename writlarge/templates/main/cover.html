New York City’s Educational Past
from the Ground Up

writlarge.ctl.columbia.edu

Writ Large tells the story of New York City’s educational past in space and time. It locates the many and varied sites of teaching and learning in the built environment that were an integral part of neighborhood life and the transformation of the city over time.

To support new historical perspectives about education and the city, Writ Large connects sites found on the map with relevant archival collections.
